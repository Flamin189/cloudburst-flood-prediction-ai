Cloud Burst Detection

Please upload a PNG, JPG, or JPEG image of clouds.
How to Use
  1. Click on the "Choose File" button and select an image of clouds from your device.
  2. Click on the "Predict" button to analyze the image.
  3. Wait for the system to process the image and provide a prediction.
  4. If a cloud burst is detected, you will receive an email alert.

What is Cloud Burst?

A cloud burst is an extreme amount of precipitation in a short period of time, sometimes accompanied by hail and thunder. It can cause flash floods and landslides.

Our system uses deep learning algorithms to analyze cloud images and detect patterns that indicate a potential cloud burst event.
